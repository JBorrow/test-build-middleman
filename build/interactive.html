Interactive Items

The concepts covered in the Nuclear and Particle Physics course are often quite challenging, and due to the nature of the course must be treat rather quickly. Hopefully these interactive things will help you understand the material better.

As always, if you have any feedback on these, or any part of the course, please contact the lecturer!

Jupyter Notebooks

These Jupyter notebooks use an interactive python shell to guide you through more data-heavy parts of the course.
